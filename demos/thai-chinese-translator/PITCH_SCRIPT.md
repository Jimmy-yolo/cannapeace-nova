# Thai-Chinese Document Translator — Pitch Script

**DEMO READY** | Setup: 5-15K THB | Subscription: 999-1,999 THB/mo

---

## Hook (3 sentences — 15 seconds)

> "Your Chinese suppliers send 10-30 invoices every month. Professional translation costs 300-800 baht per document and takes 1-3 days. This tool translates them instantly for 50-100 baht each—or unlimited for 999-1,999 baht per month."

**Pause for reaction.**

---

## Demo Flow (2 minutes)

### 1. Show Problem (15 seconds)
"Here's a typical Chinese supplier invoice." *(Show real example on phone/laptop)*

"Right now, you either:"
- Wait 1-3 days for translator (300-800 baht)
- Use Google Translate (misses business terms, not formatted)
- Ask staff with basic Chinese to manually translate (2-4 hours)

### 2. Show Solution (30 seconds)
**N6 REORDERED:** Use prospect's OWN document for demo

"Do you have a Chinese supplier invoice on your phone right now? Let me translate it live."

1. Ask prospect to send their document (LINE/WhatsApp)
2. Upload to demo URL on your phone/laptop
3. Click "Translate"
4. **Wait 30-60 seconds** *(show loading spinner, explain what's happening)*
5. Download bilingual PDF
6. **Hand them the result** - their own document, translated

**Fallback if no document:** Use pre-loaded sample invoice

### 3. Show Result (45 seconds)
*(Open generated PDF on screen)*

**Point out 3 things:**
1. **Side-by-side layout:** Chinese left, Thai right
2. **Key numbers highlighted:** Amount (จำนวนเงิน), Date (วันที่), Payment terms (เงื่อนไขการชำระเงิน)
3. **Ready for accounting:** Copy-paste amounts directly into your system

"This took 45 seconds. Professional translator: 1-3 days."

### 4. The Math (30 seconds)
"You said you get about 20 supplier invoices per month, right?"

**Current cost:**
- 20 invoices × 500 baht average = **10,000 baht/month**
- Plus 2-3 day delay on every invoice

**With this:**
- Unlimited translations: **999-1,999 baht/month**
- Instant (30-60 seconds)
- **Save 8,000+ baht monthly**

## Objection Handling

### "Can I just use Google Translate?"
"Try it — Google gives you text, but loses formatting, mixes up business terms (采购订单 vs 发票), and doesn't highlight what you need for accounting. This keeps the structure and pulls out the key numbers automatically."

### "What if the translation is wrong?"
"The first 2 weeks, I personally check every translation. If you spot an error, I fix it same day. After that, 90%+ accuracy guaranteed or I re-translate free."

### "Too expensive for just invoices"
"What does your accountant charge per hour? Most charge 500-1,000 baht. If they spend even 2 hours per month on supplier invoices, you're already paying more than this costs."

### "I'll think about it"
"Totally fair. Let me translate one document right now, free, so you have something to compare. Send me one invoice on LINE?"
