## 3. Responsible Use Guidelines

### 3.1 Start Low, Go Slow
If you are new to cannabis or trying a new strain:
- Start with a **small amount** (1-2 grams)
- Wait at least 15-30 minutes to assess effects
- Do not increase dosage rapidly

### 3.2 Safety Precautions
**Do NOT use cannabis if you plan to:**
- Drive or operate machinery
- Engage in activities requiring full alertness
- Care for children or dependents

**Store cannabis safely:**
- Keep away from children and pets
- Store in original packaging
- Keep in cool, dry place

### 3.3 Drug Interactions
Cannabis may interact with medications including:
- Blood thinners
- Sedatives
- Antidepressants
- Other prescription drugs

**Consult your doctor or pharmacist** if you take any medications.
