# Voice Greeting Scripts - All Languages

## Script Requirements
- Duration: ~10 seconds when spoken
- Tone: Sweet, young, energetic, welcoming
- Style: Friendly, inviting
- Voice: Female, warm

---

## 1. Thai (ไทย) 🇹🇭

**Script:**
```
สวัสดีค่ะ! ยินดีต้อนรับสู่แคนนาพีซ 🌿
ร้านกัญชาคุณภาพพรีเมี่ยมที่ดีที่สุดในกรุงเทพ
มีอะไรให้ช่วยคะวันนี้?
```

**Phonetic (for TTS):**
```
Sawatdee ka! Yin dee ton rap soo CannaPeace
Ran ganja kunaphap premium tee dee tee sut nai Krungthep
Mee arai hai chuay ka wan nee?
```

**Translation:**
"Hello! Welcome to CannaPeace 🌿
The best premium cannabis shop in Bangkok.
How can I help you today?"

**Duration:** ~10 seconds
**Voice tone:** ค่ะ ending (polite, feminine)

## Voice Characteristics Summary

**Personality:** Sweet, young (20s), energetic, welcoming
**Energy:** High but not overly excited
**Tone:** Warm, friendly, inviting
**Pace:** Natural, not rushed (~100-120 words per minute)
**Emotion:** Happy, helpful, eager to assist

---

## Testing Tips

1. Listen to samples before purchasing
2. Test with a short phrase first
3. Ensure pronunciation is natural
4. Check for proper intonation
5. Verify duration is 8-12 seconds
6. Make sure voice sounds genuine (not robotic)
