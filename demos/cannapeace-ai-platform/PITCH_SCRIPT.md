# Restaurant LINE-to-Excel Bridge — Pitch Script

**DEMO READY** | Setup: 3-5K THB | Subscription: 499-999 THB/mo

---

## Hook (3 sentences — 15 seconds)

> "Your restaurant gets 20-50 orders per day via LINE. Staff waste 2-4 hours manually typing each order into Excel for the kitchen and accounting. This bot does it automatically in real-time—every LINE order goes straight to your spreadsheet."

**Pause for reaction.**

---

## Demo Flow (2 minutes)

### 1. Show Problem (15 seconds)
"Right now, when a customer sends an order on LINE..." *(Show sample LINE message on phone)*

Current process:
- Staff reads LINE message
- Manually types into Excel/Google Sheets
- Copy customer name, phone, items, total
- **Time waste: 3-5 minutes per order × 30 orders = 2-3 hours/day**

### 2. Show Solution (30 seconds)
"Watch this." *(Send sample order to demo bot)*

Sample message:
```
สั่งอาหารค่ะ
- ผัดไทย 2 จาน
- ต้มยำกุ้ง 1 ถ้วย
รวม 350 บาท
ชื่อ: คุณสมชาย
โทร: 081-234-5678
```

Bot reply (instant):
```
✅ รับออเดอร์แล้วค่ะ!
👤 คุณสมชาย
💰 รวม 350 บาท
📊 บันทึกในระบบเรียบร้อย
```

### 3. Show Result (45 seconds)
*(Open Google Sheet on screen)*

**Point out 3 things:**
1. **Auto-appended:** Order appeared instantly in the sheet
2. **Parsed correctly:** Customer, phone, items, total all extracted (Thai/Chinese/English)
3. **Ready for accounting:** Copy-paste directly into your system

*(Show daily summary feature)*
```
📊 สรุปยอดวันนี้

📦 ออเดอร์: 28 รายการ
💰 ยอดรวม: 8,450 บาท

🔥 เมนูขายดี:
1. ผัดไทย (24 จาน)
2. ต้มยำกุ้ง (18 ถ้วย)
3. ข้าวผัด (15 จาน)
```

"This took 0 seconds. Your staff: 2-3 hours."

### 4. The Math (30 seconds)
"You said you get about 30 orders per day, right?"

**Current cost:**
- Staff time: 2-3 hours/day = **60-90 hours/month**
- @ 100 THB/hour = **6,000-9,000 baht/month** in wasted labor
- Plus error risk (wrong numbers, missed orders)

**With this:**
- Unlimited orders: **499-999 baht/month**
- Instant (real-time)
- **Save 5,000-8,000 baht monthly + 60-90 hours**

## Objection Handling

### "Can I just hire cheaper staff to do this?"
"Sure—but staff get tired, make mistakes, quit, need training. The bot works 24/7, never makes typos, and costs less than 1 day of minimum wage labor per month."

### "What if it parses the order wrong?"
"The first 2 weeks, I personally check every order. If you spot an error, I fix it same day. After that, 95%+ accuracy guaranteed or I re-process free. Plus you still see the LINE message, so you can catch anything weird."

### "Too expensive for just order entry"
"How much do you pay your accountant? Most charge 2,000-5,000 baht/month for bookkeeping. If this saves them even 1 hour of data entry work, you're already ahead. Plus it reduces errors that cause tax headaches."

### "I'll think about it"
"Totally fair. Let me process 5 orders right now, free, so you have something to compare. Just forward me 5 recent LINE orders?"
